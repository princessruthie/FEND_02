Brown-throated sloth

This type of sloth sleeps more than 12 hours a day and can be active day or night.

  • Scientific Name: Bradypus variegatus
  • Maxiumum Weight: 6.3 kg
  • Typical Lifespan: 30-40 years
  • Habitat: South American forests

This type of sloth has three long fingers on each limb which it uses to navigate the canopies of South America. Adult females and males can weigh up to 6.3 kg. While covered in gray-brown or beige fur, they're named for the dark patch of brown fur that spans the throat to the forehead.
